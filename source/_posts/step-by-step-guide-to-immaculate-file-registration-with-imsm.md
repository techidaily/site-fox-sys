## Properties

**Bool RegisterWithVisualStudio** \- Gets or sets the option for enabling registration with Visual Studio. Registered merge modules can be selected when creating "Setup and Deployment" projects.

**Bool RegisterWithAdvancedInstaller** \- Gets or sets the option for enabling registration with Advanced Installer. Registered merge modules can be added through the "Add Advanced Installer MSM".
